Behind the Art

Alexandria Roses

Alexandria Roses is the artist behind A Roses Art. Her work blends color, curiosity and storytelling across underwater, Southwestern, celestial and imaginative subjects.

Each collection begins with a sense of discovery: a familiar piece of nature, an unexpected detail or an ordinary moment transformed through a magical lens.
